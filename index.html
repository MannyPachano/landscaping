Year-round care

Landscaping through the seasons

Every season brings different needs. Here's a quick guide to what your Atlanta landscape requires throughout the year.

Spring

Seasonal cleanup, bed refresh, fresh mulch, and irrigation system startup for the growing season.

Summer

Weekly mowing, irrigation tuning, and the prime season for hardscape and outdoor kitchen builds.

Fall

Seasonal cleanup, leaf removal, aeration and overseeding, plus tree and shrub planting.

Winter

Drainage inspections, irrigation winterization, and planning for spring design and installation.
